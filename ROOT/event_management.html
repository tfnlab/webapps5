Welcome to Merikyan Event Management Services!

We are a full-service event planning and production company, dedicated to bringing your vision to life. Our team of experienced professionals will handle every aspect of your event, from conception to execution, ensuring that every detail is perfect and every moment is unforgettable.

Whether you are planning a corporate conference, a charity fundraiser, a wedding, or any other special occasion, we have the skills and resources to make it a success. We offer a wide range of services, including venue selection, vendor management, logistical planning, and on-site coordination.
