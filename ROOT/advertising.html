Advertising Services

Welcome to Merikyan.com, your one-stop destination for top-quality advertising services. We understand the importance of effective advertising in today's competitive market, which is why we offer a wide range of services to help businesses of all sizes reach their target audience and achieve their marketing goals.

Our team of experienced professionals has a deep understanding of the advertising industry and uses the latest technologies and strategies to create customized campaigns that deliver results. Whether you're looking to increase brand awareness, drive website traffic, or generate leads, we have the tools and expertise to help you succeed.

PPC (pay-per-click) advertising

This type of advertising allows you to bid on specific keywords and pay for each click on your ad. This can be a cost-effective way to drive targeted traffic to your website.

Social media advertising

We can help you create and manage campaigns on popular social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn, targeting specific demographics and interests to reach your ideal audience.

Display advertising

Our display ads appear on websites, apps, and other platforms, reaching a wide audience and driving brand awareness.

Email marketing

We can help you design and send engaging email campaigns to your target audience, with the goal of driving conversions and sales.

Video advertising

Video has become an increasingly popular medium for marketing and advertising, and we can help you create and distribute high-quality video ads that capture the attention of your target audience. This can include video ads on social media platforms, YouTube, and other video streaming sites.

Influencer marketing

We can help you identify and collaborate with influential people in your industry or target market who can help promote your products or services to their followers. This can be a highly effective way to reach a new audience and increase brand awareness.
